How To Play

* Enter the names of the players and submit.

* Roll the dice as many times as you want to but do so at your own risk. If you roll a 1,then no points are accumulated in that particular round and your turn is over

* If you hold before rolling a 1,the points accumulated within that round are tallied and added to your score.

* Reach 100 points before your opponent and be declared the winner.
